Instrucciones:
para compilar java: javac com/medicalsoft/..../*.java
para crear jar: jar cvf "nombre".jar com/medicalsoft/.../*.class
para limpiar la carpeta: rmdir /s /q com\medicalsoft\...

1. javac com/medicalsoft/rrhh/model/*.java
2. jar cvf medicalsoftRRHHModel.jar com/medicalsoft/rrhh/model/*.class
E: rmdir /s /q com\medicalsoft\rrhh\model

3. javac -cp medicalsoftRRHHModel.jar com/medicalsoft/infraestructura/model/*.java
4. jar cvf medicalsoftInfraModel.jar com/medicalsoft/infraestructura/model/*.class
E: rmdir /s /q com\medicalsoft\infraestructura\model

5. javac -cp medicalsoftRRHHModel.jar;medicalsoftInfraModel.jar com/medicalsoft/servicios/model/*.java
6. jar cvf medicalsoftServModel.jar com/medicalsoft/servicios/model/*.class
E: rmdir /s /q com\medicalsoft\servicios\model

7. javac -cp medicalsoftRRHHModel.jar;medicalsoftInfraModel.jar;medicalsoftServModel.jar com/medicalsoft/program/main/*.java
8. jar cvf medicalsoftPrincipal.jar com/medicalsoft/program/main/*.class (NO PIDEN COMPONENTE .jar normal)
8 (jar ejecutable: con MANIFEST): jar cvfm medicalsoftPrincipal.jar MANIFEST.MF com/medicalsoft/program/main/*.class
E: rmdir /s /q com\medicalsoft\program\main

Probar: java -jar medicalsoftPrincipal.jar
